About animals

Braving bloodsucking leeches and the blazing Malaysian sun,
four volunteers trudge along the heavily forested Marcus trail
in Malaysia’s Sungai Yu ecological corridor, which plays a crucial
role in connecting the two largest forested landscapes in the country
– the Titiwangsa mountains and the 130-million-year-old Taman Negara rainforest,
the largest national park in the country.
